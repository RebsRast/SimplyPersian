Spices & Seasonings

Cardamom

Cardamom has a strong, unique taste, with an intensely aromatic, resinous fragrance. Black cardamom has a distinctly more smokey, though not bitter, aroma, with a coolness some consider similar to mint.

Benefits

  1. Digestion
  2. Antioxidant
  3. Hiccups
  4. Aphrodisiac
  5. Cold and Flu
  6. Oral Health

Cumin

Cumin seeds are pungent, potent little things with the ability to significantly change the trajectory of a dish.

Benefits

  1. Reduces blood glucose
  2. Reduces total cholesterol
  3. Improves immune system

Omani

Omani - aka black limes - give your cooking new life! These dried limes are one of those ingredients that turns your dishes from blah to wow!

Benefits

  1. Combat aging skin
  2. Help with post-menopausal women
  3. Anti-carcinogenic
  4. Prevents formation of Kidney Stones
  5. Lowers cholesterol

Saffron

For the most part, it is difficult to label the flavor of saffron, since its complexity is experienced by everyone a little differently. Some say saffron has a faint honey flavor, while others swear it tastes like the sea.

Benefits

  1. Asthma
  2. Cough
  3. Insomnia
  4. Anti-carcinogenic
  5. Intestinal gas
  6. Depression
  7. Alzheimer's disease
  8. Heartburn
  9. Dry skin
